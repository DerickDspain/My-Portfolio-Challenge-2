About Me:

My name is Derick Dspain, I currently reside in a rural south Texas town called Uvalde. I was raised on a cattle ranch learning the trade of a cattleman along the way. Growing up on a ranch has provided me with the mindset that I apply today in my pursuit of knowledge in coding. Whether it be solving complex problems and innovating new ways to solve them, or managing my time to complete a project on time and correctly meeting the criteria needed. Outside of work I enjoy anything pertaining to the outdoors, I enjoy online PC games with friends, most physical sports, and I am an avid hunter and fisherman as well. In the future I hope to apply my knowledge and ideas to help the agricultural industry grow and achieve new heights. My goals in life are fueled by my family, my desire to learn new and interesting things, a good challenge, and just the overall knowledge and experience that one needs in life.
